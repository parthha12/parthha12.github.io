Story

Three brothers whose names spell the product — Jacquavius (J) organizes, Octavius (O) interacts, Taquavion (T) resurfaces. One loop (J → O → T) behind a single chat door; the policy decides whether anything interrupts you.

Features

Say when in plain language (⌘N): time, app-open, screen phrase, or when on slack, …. Find offline (⌘P); search your screen history; nudge anytime (⌘⇧R); crew chat (⌘⇧P) to file, explain, or tune — one calm overlay, silence by default.

Purpose

Capture anything once on your Mac and get it back in the right app at the right moment — for people who context-switch all day and keep losing follow-ups between tools, without another wiki or notification flood.

Who it's for

Built for people who live in context switches

Solo founders, engineers, designers, and consultants who bounce between Cursor, Slack, GitHub, Zoom, and Figma — and keep losing follow-ups between apps. Jot is the when-to-surface layer, not another wiki.

Best use case

“Remind me when I’m back in the right app.” Capture once with ⌘N, let Jacquavius set the trigger, talk to Octavius if you need clarity, and let Taquavion + the policy bring one card when Slack, GitHub, or your doc matches.

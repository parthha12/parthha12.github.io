The J·O·T Crew

Three brothers.
One scratchpad.

Same house, different jobs. You talk to whoever fits the moment — not a generic AI assistant.

J

Jacquavius

The c is for sophisticated.

Reads everything you captured and teaches it back plain. With the crew he's down-to-earth; alone he's nerdy with a book. Rich, calm about it — still street if you look close.

  • Read & teach
  • Explain your stash
  • Connect the thread

"Meet you where you are."

O

Octavius

He watches. You get the note when it matters.

Keeps watch on app, screen, and time — then taps you when the note you delegated is actually relevant. The corner overlay is his hand on your shoulder.

  • App-open reminders
  • Timing & snooze
  • Policy feedback

"I watch so you don't have to."

T

Taquavion

Secretary energy. Talk, don't tap.

The brother who closes the loop. After Jacquavius makes meaning and Octavius handles timing, Taquavion files what stays — folders, library hygiene, standing rules.

  • File & sort
  • Library cleanup
  • Standing rules

"Say it, I'll handle it."
